Privacy Policy

1. Introduction 
Welcome to ID.Amura. This Privacy Policy will help you understand how we collect, use, and protect your personal information. By using our services, you consent to the processing of your information as set forth in this policy.


2. Information Collection 
When you register for an account, we collect personal information such as your name, email address, and other necessary details. The information collected is stored in a non-custodial wallet, empowering you with control over your data.


3. Use of Information 
We use your personal information to provide, improve, and personalize our services, to contact you about your account, for Know Your Customer (KYC) processes, and for other legitimate business purposes.


4. User Control of Information 
As part of our commitment to your privacy, we empower you to decide what information you want to share with the Amura Business Ecosystem partners. You can review, change, or delete your personal information at any time by logging into your account.


5. Information Sharing and Disclosure 
We do not sell your personal information. We only share your personal information with other Amura Business Ecosystem partners if you authorize us to do so, and only as necessary to provide our services or as required by law.


6. Data Security 
We leverage blockchain technology and a variety of security measures to protect your personal information. These include data encryption, secure server environments, and other technical and physical measures.


7. User Rights and Special Legal Frameworks 
We do NOT sell information.
If you are a California resident, you can also refer to the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). For more information, you can visit the official CCPA information site:  https://oag.ca.gov/privacy/ccpa.

For European Union residents, you have certain rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). You can learn more about these rights by visiting the EU's official GDPR information site: https://gdpr-info.eu/.

If you wish to exercise any of these rights, or if you have any questions regarding your privacy rights, please contact us at privacypolicy@amura.io.


8. Third-Party Websites and Services 
Our services may include links to third-party websites or services that are not owned or controlled by us. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party websites or services.


9. Data Retention 
We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. The Amura Business Ecosystem partners with whom you share your information may also retain this data as required by their legal obligations.


10. Children's Privacy 
Our services are not intended for use by children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.


11. Changes to the Privacy Policy 
We may change this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post any changes on this page and, if the changes are significant, we will provide a more prominent notice.


12. Contact Information 
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacypolicy@amura.io.
